Privacy Policy

App: Word Chain Rush
Platform: iOS

Last updated: April 8, 2026

We keep it simple. Word Chain Rush does not collect, store or share your personal data. Your scores and settings stay on your device.

Introduction

Word Chain Rush ("the App") is developed and published by Driftline Games. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App on iOS.

Data We Collect

We do not directly collect or store any personal data from users of this App.

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

The App uses Firebase Firestore to store world leaderboard scores. Only your display name, country flag, and score are submitted — no account or identity information is required.

Local Storage

Your scores, settings, language preference, and display name are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to Driftline Games.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment or financial information.

Advertising

The free version of the App displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your Advertising Identifier (IDFA) to show personalised ads. You can limit ad tracking in iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Children

The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
