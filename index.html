Word Chain Rush

iOS  ·  Free  ·  Word Game

Chain words, build combos, beat the world. Type a word starting with the last letter of the previous word — and don't let the timer run out.

Features
🔥

Combo System

Chain words to build combos. Hit x25 for GODLIKE, x50 for LEGENDARY — see how far you can push.

🌍

World Leaderboard

Compete in real-time against players worldwide. Your best score goes on the global Top 100.

🗣️

5 Languages

Play in English, Norwegian, German, French or Spanish — word lists and UI fully localised.

💀

Hard Mode

10 second start, no shields, 1.5× points. For those who think normal mode is too easy.

🏆

17 Achievements

From Chain Starter to The Last Word — unlock them all and prove you're a word legend.

🛡️

Shield System

Shields absorb mistakes and keep your streak alive. Earn more at milestone combos.
